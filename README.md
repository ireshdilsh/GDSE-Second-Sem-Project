
<h1>Project Overview</h1>
<h5>Lexora is a web-based platform inspired by Medium that allows users to read, write, and share articles seamlessly. It serves as a digital space for individuals to express their ideas, stories, and expertise while engaging with a community of readers and writers.</h5>
